Alignment quality & statistics
==============================

- :doc:`Alignment entropy <../commands/alignment_entropy>` - Shannon entropy across alignment sites
- :doc:`Alignment length <../commands/alignment_length>` - Length of an input alignment
- :doc:`Alignment length no gaps <../commands/alignment_length_no_gaps>` - Alignment length excluding gapped sites
- :doc:`Alignment outlier regions <../commands/alignment_outlier_regions>` - Detect localized outlier regions in multiple sequence alignments
- :doc:`Alignment outlier taxa <../commands/alignment_outlier_taxa>` - Identify outlier taxa in alignments
- :doc:`Codon dN/dS <../commands/codon_dnds>` - Pairwise synonymous and nonsynonymous substitution-rate estimates
- :doc:`Column score <../commands/column_score>` - Column score for alignment quality
- :doc:`Composition per taxon <../commands/composition_per_taxon>` - Nucleotide or amino acid composition per taxon
- :doc:`Compositional bias per site <../commands/compositional_bias_per_site>` - Detect compositional bias across sites
- :doc:`Evolutionary Rate per Site <../commands/evolutionary_rate_per_site>` - Site-specific evolutionary rate estimation
- :doc:`Guanine-cytosine (GC) content <../commands/gc_content>` - GC content of an alignment
- :doc:`Identity matrix <../commands/identity_matrix>` - Pairwise sequence identity heatmap
- :doc:`Occupancy per taxon <../commands/occupancy_per_taxon>` - Taxon occupancy in alignment columns
- :doc:`Pairwise identity <../commands/pairwise_identity>` - Pairwise sequence identity in an alignment
- :doc:`Parsimony informative sites <../commands/parsimony_informative_sites>` - Count parsimony informative sites
- :doc:`Plot alignment QC <../commands/plot_alignment_qc>` - Visual quality control plots for alignments
- :doc:`Relative composition variability <../commands/relative_composition_variability>` - Composition variability across taxa
- :doc:`Relative composition variability, taxon <../commands/relative_composition_variability_taxon>` - Per-taxon relative composition variability
- :doc:`Sum-of-pairs score <../commands/sum_of_pairs_score>` - Sum-of-pairs alignment quality score
- :doc:`Variable sites <../commands/variable_sites>` - Count variable sites in an alignment
