Alignment & dataset utilities
=============================

- :doc:`Alignment recoding <../commands/alignment_recoding>` - Recode alignment into reduced alphabets
- :doc:`Alignment subsampling <../commands/alignment_subsample>` - Randomly subsample genes, partitions, or sites
- :doc:`Create concatenation matrix <../commands/create_concatenation_matrix>` - Concatenate multiple alignments into a supermatrix
- :doc:`Faidx <../commands/faidx>` - Extract entries from FASTA files
- :doc:`Mask alignment <../commands/mask_alignment>` - Mask sites in an alignment
- :doc:`Occupancy filter <../commands/occupancy_filter>` - Filter alignments or trees by cross-file taxon occupancy (works with both FASTA and Newick)
- :doc:`Protein-to-nucleotide alignment <../commands/thread_dna>` - Thread nucleotide onto protein alignment
- :doc:`Rename FASTA entries <../commands/rename_fasta_entries>` - Rename entries in a FASTA file
- :doc:`Taxon groups <../commands/taxon_groups>` - Group alignment or tree files by shared taxon sets (works with both FASTA and Newick)
