Tree summary statistics
=======================

- :doc:`Degree of violation of the molecular clock <../commands/degree_of_violation_of_a_molecular_clock>` - Measure molecular clock violation

- :doc:`Tip-to-tip node distance <../commands/tip_to_tip_node_distance>` - Node distance between two tips
- :doc:`Total tree length <../commands/total_tree_length>` - Sum of all branch lengths
